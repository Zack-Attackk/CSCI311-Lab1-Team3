Destinations you wont want to miss.

With natural beauty of this caliber, you've got to see it to believe it.

Neck Point

Rain or shine, a walk around the ever scenic Neck Point Park is sure to impress with it's winding trails, stunning views, and fresh ocean air.

With beaches to comb, often windy skies for kite flying, and picnic tables for lunch, Neck Point is a lovely way to spend a day.

Neck Point's City of Nanaimo info page

Waterfront and Maffeo Sutton Park

Head downtown and you'll be treated to town's namesake; the Harbour!

Once there, take a pleasent stroll along the water front, visiting unique and original shops and snack spots along the way.

Visit Maffeo Sutton Park; with it's playground and swimable Swy-a-Lana Lagoon (the park's famous manmade lake), and picnic tables, there's more than enough fun to fill your day.

Maffeo Sutton Park's City of Nanaimo info page

Mount Benson

Feeling adventurous? If so, a hike up Mount Beson is sure to fit the bill!

Though the slope is steep, the increible view at the top rewards those who can persevere.

Just be sure to pack lunch and plenty of water if you do plan to go all the way up!
